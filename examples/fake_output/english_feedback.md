## 🚀 Top 3 Improvement Areas

1. **Indefinite Articles with Roles & Singular Nouns:** Ensure `a/an` is placed before professional titles and singular countable nouns (`a backend developer`, `a microservice`, `a problem`).
2. **Present Perfect Continuous for Duration:** Transition from `"I working with Python for five years"` to `"I have been working with Python for five years"` when discussing ongoing career experience.
3. **Third-Person Singular Verb Agreement:** Pay attention to third-person singular `-s` endings and auxiliary verbs (`"PostgreSQL was get very slow"` → `"PostgreSQL became very slow"` / `"database don't crash"` → `"database doesn't crash"`).
