# Essential Technical & Professional Vocabulary Table

| Word / Phrase | Professional Definition | Example Sentence (Interview Context) | Target Level |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :---: |
| **Connection Pooling** | A technique of keeping open database connections in a cache so they can be reused across multiple requests without re-establishing connections. | *"We configured **connection pooling** via PgBouncer to prevent our PostgreSQL instance from running out of available sockets."* | C1 |
| **Read Replicas** | Read-only database instances that sync from the master database to offload heavy query traffic. | *"To scale our reporting dashboard, we directed all analytical queries to dedicated **read replicas**."* | B2 |
| **Decouple** | Separating interconnected components or systems so they can operate, scale, and fail independently. | *"Our primary architecture goal was to **decouple** the ingestion webhook queue from the main relational database."* | C1 |
| **Latency Degradation** | The progressive slowing down of network response times under heavy load or poor routing. | *"By implementing Redis caching, we successfully prevented **latency degradation** during peak holiday traffic."* | C1 |
| **Asynchronous Workflows** | Team communication and operational processes that do not require immediate, real-time responses. | *"Because our team spans three continents, we rely heavily on well-documented **asynchronous workflows**."* | B2 |
| **Proactive Optimization** | Identifying and resolving potential system bottlenecks before they impact end users. | *"I prefer **proactive optimization** through continuous monitoring over reactive hot-fixing after an outage."* | C1 |
